Production Workflow

  1. Link Esri account, OSM username, email, and wallet.
  2. Submit an OSM changeset/element ID.
  3. Backend validates public OSM object and prevents duplicate claims.
  4. Unverified or high-value claims go to DAO/community review.
  5. Approved claims are paid in GEOW tokens through the rewards oracle.
